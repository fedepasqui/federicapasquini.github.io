    I’m Federica, a 23-year-old designer from Bergamo, a small town in Italy. Ever since I had possession of a computer, I liked putting together videos and music to communicate a message,
    but especially to move those who were watching. This is how I ended up studying design, discovering more aspects that I love about this field. About my creative process, I never let
    an idea alone, I always try to develop it and explore it in multiple different ways until it “clicks”.
