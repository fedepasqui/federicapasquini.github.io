    THE CHALLENGE

    Reflect on our daily actions through a speculative design project


    ROLE

    Design concept, Branding, Copywriting,
    Packaging design, Video production and editing


    ALSO ON

    Instagram, Brand Website, Showcase

    Mor is a speculative design project that reflects on the awareness of the effective impact caused by daily actions.
    The setting is a future scenario where paper is continuously decreasing, causing the necessity to control its consumption. The Treem device resolves the problem
    by supplying the required toilet sheets after the credit card
    is inserted.

    However, to provoke the public even more,
    the price is exagerated and the paper is produced out of endangered trees, each identified by
    a graphic texture on the roll. They are divided
    in three price ranges: the more it costs,
    the more rare the tree is.

    In other words, Mor is a fictional brand that, with an unexpected ironic twist, puts overconsumption above sustainability
    and has an anti-enviromental messaging. The aim of the speculation is
    to reflect
    on the current mindset and practices concerning the environment.

    This project has been selected for the showcase
    Design X Designer at Politecnico di Milano
    on the occasion of Fuori Salone 2022.
